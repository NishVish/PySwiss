Role Overview – Jr. Data Analyst & Developer I am responsible for designing, developing, and implementing a centralized data management and reporting system for the organization. The company was previously operating using Excel-based processes, and I am leading the transition to a structured SQL-based database system to improve data accuracy, operational efficiency, reporting, and financial tracking. What I Do (Functional Overview) 1️⃣ System Development & Implementation Designed and implemented the company’s core database structure. Built a centralized system to manage: Company records (exhibitors) Contacts and communication Leads and stall bookings Event data Invoices and payments Structured data relationships to avoid duplication and manual errors. Created backup and audit tracking mechanisms. Impact: Reduced dependency on multiple Excel files. Improved data consistency and traceability. Enabled scalable system growth for future exhibitions. 2️⃣ Excel to SQL Migration Analyzed existing Excel workflows. Cleaned and standardized legacy data. Migrated company, contact, and financial data into structured database tables. Eliminated duplicate and inconsistent records. Created validation logic to ensure data accuracy. Impact: Centralized all historical data. Improved data reliability. Reduced manual reconciliation work. 3️⃣ Lead & Exhibition Management System Built system modules to manage: Lead creation and tracking Stall allocation and pricing Booking forms Exhibitor status tracking Implemented structured lead lifecycle tracking. Impact: Clear visibility of booking pipeline. Better tracking of exhibitor confirmations. Reduced miscommunication between departments. 4️⃣ Financial & Payment Tracking Developed invoice and payment tracking structure. Implemented GST calculation tracking. Built system to track pending vs completed payments. Enabled invoice-payment reconciliation. Impact: Improved revenue tracking accuracy. Better monitoring of outstanding payments. Reduced financial discrepancies. 5️⃣ Data Analysis & Reporting Generate business reports including: Lead conversion Revenue per event Payment status reports Sales performance tracking Support management with data-driven insights. Identify inconsistencies and suggest improvements. Impact: Improved decision-making through structured reporting. Reduced manual reporting effort. Increased visibility into financial and operational performance. 6️⃣ Data Governance & Audit Implemented audit logs for updates. Structured role-based user management. Created cross-validation tracking mechanisms. Ensured historical data backup availability. Impact: Improved accountability. Better data security and tracking. Reduced risk of data loss. Summary of My Contribution I am: Designing and developing the company’s core data infrastructure. Transitioning operations from Excel-based processes to a structured database system. Creating automation and reporting mechanisms. Supporting business operations with accurate analytics. Improving financial tracking and operational transparency. This role combines: Database development Business process structuring Data analysis System implementation Operational optimization
